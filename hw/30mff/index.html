More About 30MFF

The 30 minute film festival is a biannual film festival held at New York University Abu Dhabi. Leaders from around the globe who have opted to take Communications Lab engage in a highly demanding activity. Participants are given 30 move around the globe and shoot scenes, and an additional 30 minutes to bring them together to form a short film. It is indeed an incredible sight to watch amateur filmmakers create some of the world's best films. The films are watched by audiences from across the globe and are highly appreciated.

The participants are then tasked with creating a website for their short film, which also turn out to be award winning websites, appreciated for both their elegant design and meaningful content.
